Extempers need to be caught up on the news. Here are some of my favorite sources:

New York Times - set this as your browser's homepage, often provided for free by your school
Washington Post - reliable domestic source, goes on sale on Cyber Monday
Instapaper - not a source, but filters out images and dodges paywalls for most sites
The Atlantic - amazing long-form journalism, paywall dodgeable with Instapaper
The New Yorker - thought provoking long reads
Vox - short, sweet, simple. if you don't know much about an issue, Vox will explain it very well
FiveThirtyEight - the best data-driven reporting out there
PBS NewsHour - no time to read? listen to this every day during your commute
The Economist - good for economics (duh)

For international news:
Foreign Affairs
Foreign Policy

Miscellaneous Sources
Reuters Oddly Enough - excellent for creating funny intros
